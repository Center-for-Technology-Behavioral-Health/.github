# Center for Technology & Behavioral Health (CTBH)  
A research center at the Geisel School of Medicine at Dartmouth College  
[https://www.c4tbh.org/](https://www.c4tbh.org/)

---

## Mission  
We use science to inform the development, evaluation, and implementation of digital tools for behavioral health.

---

## About Us  
The Center for Technology & Behavioral Health (CTBH) is dedicated to identifying and studying state-of-the-art issues related to digital therapeutics for behavioral health: rigorous evaluation of interventions, improvement of research and implementation methodologies, and translating evidence into practice.

---

## Our Focus Areas  
- Research Studies: State-of-the-art technologies and interventions for behavioral health.  
- Program Reviews & Digital Health Resources: Synthesizing current evidence for digital health tools.  
- Implementation Research: Bridging the gap between innovation and real-world practice.  
- Emerging Data & Analytics: Leveraging new technologies and data to inform behavioral health solutions.  
- Pilot Innovations: Seed funding and early-stage work to incubate promising digital tools.

---

## Why It Matters  
Digital interventions for behavioral health offer scalable, accessible, and innovative ways to support individuals and communities facing substance use disorders, co-occurring conditions, and other behavioral health challenges. By advancing the science of digital therapeutics, CTBH helps ensure these tools are effective, equitable, and ready for real-world adoption.
